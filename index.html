Interests

Within the field of video game development, my particular interest centers around the exploration of advanced technologies with a focus on their practical applications. Specifically, I am intrigued by the potentials presented by virtual reality, artificial intelligence, and serious games.´
Currently, I am immersed in the development of a significant project as part of my final degree undertaking. This project is designed to provide assistance to children dealing with dyslexia in their daily lives.
This project underscores my commitment to harnessing technology for the greater good of society. Through innovative solutions, I aspire to lend support to children facing challenges associated with dyslexia. This project represents my dedication to utilizing video game development as a channel for creative expression and as a catalyst for constructive transformations in the lives of those in need.
